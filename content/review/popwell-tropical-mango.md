Medium sweet. After a very dull start a rush of sweetness and flavor arrives in waves. The first wave of fruit is fairly rich and forms a solid core with hints of coconut near the end. The second wave is fairly sour and fairly reminiscent of a kombucha. The third wave is a slightly odd chemical finish that is hard to place. Might be the Stevia, the apple juice, or any of the other ingredients that make this a "functional" soft drink. The finish is almost absurdly long with the sour tones dominant. Pass.

Purchased from [Cub Roseville](https://www.cub.com/)
