Quite sweet. Almost exactly what one would expect from the name. Strong cinnamon over fruity apple with some allspice, vanilla, and maybe just a little clove. The spice mix has a tiny bit of kick and blends really well overall. Not subtle, but not overpowered either. Highly recommended.

Purchased from [Urban Growler Brewing](https://www.urbangrowlerbrewing.com/)
