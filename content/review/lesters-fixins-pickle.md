Very sweet. Taking a sniff before sipping you would think that you are in for something pretty close to just drinking out of a jar of hamburger dill slices. The taste though is so much more sweet that it startles slightly, even though it's fully expected from a Rocket Fizz novelty. Classic lemon-lime start with dill starting in the middle, quickly blooming before the surprisingly pleasant medium length finish. The combination of lime and dill in that finish is actually quite nice, as long as you like the flavor of dill. Never really a vinegar tang, instead relying on the slight acid of the citrus. Ok.
