Very sweet. Not subtle with their flavors, which is unusual with a "natural soda". Artificial blueberry from start to medium long finish. More tartness in the start than I expected, possibly enough to be considered "sour" by some, which dissapates relatively quickly giving way to sugary berries. Plenty of industrial echoes throughout are more present than I would like, though subtle enough to be missed if you aren't looking for them. Not my thing, but not terrible. Ok

Purchased at [Tim and Tom's Speedy Market](https://www.timandtomsspeedymarket.com/).
