Not sweet. Gin drinkers might find this one particularly interesting. Herbal and lightly salty start doesn't give a lot of clear indications of individual flavors. Lime is clearly present, but mostly as a background that the other flavors interact with. Some umami in the middle lasts into the subtle and slightly long end. Unusual and a nice change from sweeter drinks. Ok

Purchased at [Tim and Tom's Speedy Market](https://www.timandtomsspeedymarket.com/).
