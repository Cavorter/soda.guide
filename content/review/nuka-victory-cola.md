Very sweet. Peach is primary in the start and lasts well into the middle where the mango finally catches up and largely takes over the extremely long and sticky finish. Both fruits flirt with natural tones though ultimately come through with more artificial tones overall, for better or worse. Generally fairly nice and easy to spot as a Jones flavor. Ok.
