Quite sweet. Mild spearmint start with some fruit undertones develops just a hint of sharpness with a very round mouthfeel. The spearmint resolves to cucumber as the long finish arrives and lingers. Very lightly carbonated. A great alternative to something like a mint julep in the height of summer. Recommended

Purchased from [Urban Growler Brewing](https://www.urbangrowlerbrewing.com/)
