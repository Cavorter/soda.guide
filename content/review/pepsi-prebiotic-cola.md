Very sweet. Like regular Pepsi, but generally worse. The very start has most of the notes of a regular sugared Pepsi until the Stevia hits and it gets just kind of weird. The essential flavor profile remains, but with a slightly sour backdrop and slightly actinic foreground in an agonizignly long finish. I haven't had a Zero Sugar variant for awhile so it's hard to compare, but I do think it's safe to say that if you wanted a lower calorie Pepsi, that direction is going to be generally a much better idea. Pass
