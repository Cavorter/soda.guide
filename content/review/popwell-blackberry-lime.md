Very sweet. Very berry start is sweet and full with some tartness and citrus arriving with a wave toward the middle. The sweetener blend works possibly better with this flavor than any of the others in this brand I've tried, though still slightly off putting. Fruit trends toward artificial in the exceedingly long finish. Ok.

Purchased from [Cub Roseville](https://www.cub.com/)
